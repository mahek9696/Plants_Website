Researchers have conducted various studies to understand the benefits of bringing plants indoors3. If you already keep indoor plants, you’re probably already a fan of the benefits your plants provide.
One of the major contributions plants provide is cleaning the air. Basic Biology teaches us that plants take in carbon dioxide and release oxygen. This oxygen is essential to our survival.
Apart from this essential role, plants also influence our overall well-being. A study revealed that indoor plants positively affect the human autonomic nervous system. The plants have this effect by suppressing sympathetic activity1.
